Yummy Oatmeal Cookies

Oatmeal cookies are a prize staple in many households. Along with chocholate chip cookies it can be eaten during anytime of the year. Keep reading to find out how to make these baked oats of goodness!

Things You'll Need

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon baking
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • li
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 softened butter
  • 1 cup of packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup of white sugar

Instructions

  1. Whisk butter, both brown sugar and white sugar, egg, and vanilla together.
  2. Stir in oats and optional ingredients if you are using them
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  4. Drop dough by teaspoon on an ungreased cookie sheet, parchment paper, or silpat and bake.
  5. Bake for 13 minutes
  6. Let sit for 5 minutes to cool and harden
  7. Enjoy!
